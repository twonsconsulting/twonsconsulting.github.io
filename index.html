Agentic AI

We design and build AI agent systems that automate complex, multi-step workflows that previously required extensive manual effort. Our solutions connect AI models to your existing infrastructure — databases, document stores, dashboards — so they can act autonomously while keeping humans in the loop for critical decisions.

Capabilities

  • Model Context Protocol (MCP) frameworks connecting AI agents to Power BI, SQL databases, and enterprise systems
  • Document ingestion pipelines that extract, curate, and validate data automatically
  • Natural language interfaces for querying structured data and archived records
  • Causal process flow construction for policy analysis and complex interventions
  • Multi-agent orchestration for evaluation, scoring, and decision-support workflows

Representative Work

  • AI-powered technology evaluation and triage system for an investment accelerator
  • Automated literature and data mining system for government research archives
  • Document-to-database pipelines with human-in-the-loop validation
